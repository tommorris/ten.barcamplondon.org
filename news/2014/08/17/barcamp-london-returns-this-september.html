BarCamp London returns this September

After a three year hiatus, during which we've organised two BarCamp Berkshires and one epic-scale hackday, we are thrilled to announce that BarCamp finally returns to London for the 10th time: BarCamp London X!

Microsoft has very kindly offered to host us on Saturday 27th September and Sunday 28th September at their offices in Central London, located a short distance from London Victoria Railway Station.
